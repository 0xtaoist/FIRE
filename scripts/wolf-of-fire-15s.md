# WOLF OF FIRE — 15 Second Script

**Format:** Short-form vertical video (TikTok / Reels / Shorts)
**Runtime:** 15 seconds
**Reference energy:** *Wolf of Wall Street* trailer — chaotic, ~25–30 cuts, chest-thump chant, money flying, crowd screaming, mid-air jumps, quick-fire punchlines.
**Character (FIRE GUY):** Balding, messy rust-orange spiked hair, patchy stubble, heavy-lidded eyes, smirk. Dark navy fleece vest over white collared shirt, small flame logo on the chest. **Every single shot must match the reference portrait — same face, same vest, same flame.**

---

## ACT 1 — "THE BEFORE" (0:00 – 0:02) — 2 SECONDS FLAT

Desaturated. Gray. Dead silence except fluorescent hum.

**[0:00 – 0:01]** *Cubicle. FIRE GUY slumped at a spreadsheet. Dead eyes. Tie crooked.*

> **FIRE GUY** *(flat, beaten)*
> "Retire at sixty-five, I guess…"

**[0:01 – 0:02]** *Calendar rip: 2030 → 2040 → 2050. His face getting older, grayer, sadder in 3 sub-frames.*

> **V.O. (whisper):** *"…there has to be more."*

---

## ACT 2 — "THE IGNITION" (0:02 – 0:04) — 2 SECONDS

**MATCH STRIKE SFX. Screen burns away like paper.**

**[0:02]** *CLOSE-UP: phone screen — flame logo ignites. Reflected in his pupils.*
**[0:02.5]** *Whip-pan to his face — the reference-portrait SMIRK snaps on. Color floods in.*
**[0:03]** *Index fund chart ROCKETS off the top of the screen, leaves a fire trail.*
**[0:03.3]** *He rips his tie off in one yank.*
**[0:03.6]** *SLAMS his laptop shut. "I QUIT" sticky note smacks onto the monitor.*
**[0:04]** **ON-SCREEN TEXT, huge:** **F.I.R.E.**

> **FIRE GUY** *(building)*
> "Wait — I can retire at THIRTY-TWO?!"

---

## ACT 3 — "WOLF OF FIRE" (0:04 – 0:14) — 10 SECONDS OF PURE CHAOS

Full saturation. Gold-hour blasting. Bass kicks in. **~20 rapid-fire cuts.** Every 0.3–0.5s a new shot. Crowd chant under everything: **"FIRE! FIRE! FIRE!"**

### Block A — The Declaration (0:04 – 0:06)

**[0:04.2]** *FIRE GUY on a stage, mic in hand, spotlight, crowd of office-drones losing their minds.*

> **FIRE GUY** *(SCREAMING, Belfort cadence)*
> "I'M NEVER GOING BACK TO THAT CUBICLE!"

**[0:05]** *Low-angle hero shot — he throws a fistful of RECEIPTS in the air like cash. They rain down in slow-mo.*
**[0:05.4]** *Crowd POV — hundreds of phones held up, every screen glowing with the flame logo, like lighters at a concert.*
**[0:05.8]** *Cutaway: stock portfolio up 847% — green candles exploding.*

### Block B — The Party (0:06 – 0:10)

Trailer-chaos montage. Hard cuts on the beat. FIRE GUY in all of them — same face, same vest, same flame.

**[0:06.0]** *FIRE GUY on a rooftop, pouring a six-pack of the cheapest beer into a champagne tower.* (The FIRE joke: frugal wealth.)
**[0:06.4]** *Slow-mo chest-thump — he pounds the flame logo on his vest three times, mouth open in a war-yell.* **"UNGH. UNGH. UNGH."**
**[0:06.8]** *Cut: he does a running knee-slide across a conference table, scattering printed expense reports like confetti.*
**[0:07.2]** *Suitcase bursts open — instead of cash, it's VANGUARD index fund statements blowing in the wind.*
**[0:07.6]** *Coworker dumping a coffee mug into a trash can — MATCH-CUT to FIRE GUY torching his old tie on a grill.*
**[0:08.0]** *Crowd lifts him onto their shoulders. He raises both arms, vest flame roaring up past his collar.*
**[0:08.4]** *Jump cut: he's dancing on a desk, HR lady in the background just nodding, resigned.*
**[0:08.8]** *Clothesline of dress shirts going up in a single WHOOOSH of flame.*
**[0:09.2]** *Honda Civic burnout in a parking lot — tires smoking. He leans out the window, smirk locked in.* (Not a Lambo. He's FIRE, not Belfort.)
**[0:09.6]** *Back to stage — confetti cannon of **dollar bills AND coupons** blasts over the crowd.*

### Block C — The Sermon (0:10 – 0:13)

Chant drops out. Silence. He walks to the edge of the stage. Tight close-up — **EXACT framing of the reference portrait.**

**[0:10.0 – 0:10.8]** *Close-up. Same heavy-lidded eyes. Same smirk. Vest flame burning real now, licking up his neck. He doesn't flinch.*

> **FIRE GUY** *(low, slow, Belfort-venom)*
> "They told me I'd retire… at **sixty-five**."

**[0:10.8]** *Single cut: calendar snapping in half.*

> **FIRE GUY**
> "I retired… at **thirty-two**."

**[0:11.6]** *He points a finger straight down the lens.*

> **FIRE GUY** *(rising)*
> "Cut the lattes. Crank the savings. **LIGHT. IT. UP.**"

**[0:12.0 / 0:12.4 / 0:12.8]** *Three hard cuts on "LIGHT-IT-UP":*
  - Fist pump / flame erupts behind him.
  - Wide shot of entire crowd jumping in unison.
  - Back to the portrait close-up — pupils now full flames.

### Block D — The Button (0:13 – 0:15)

Bass cuts. Near-silence. Just the crackle of fire.

**[0:13.0 – 0:14.2]** *Portrait framing. He leans in. Whispers.*

> **FIRE GUY**
> "You're next."

**[0:14.2]** **SMASH CUT TO BLACK.**
**[0:14.4]** *Flame logo ignites center-frame with a WHOOMP.*
**[0:14.8 – 0:15.0]** **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** **JOIN THE FIRE.**

## Production Notes

- **Character lock:** every shot = reference portrait. Balding crown, rust-orange spiked tufts, stubble, heavy-lidded eyes, navy fleece, white collar, flame chest logo. Do not restyle him in any cutaway.
- **Color arc:** Act 1 desaturated gray → Act 2 color floods in on the match strike → Act 3 fully blown-out golden-hour saturation.
- **Audio arc:** fluorescent hum → match strike SFX → bass drop at 0:04 → crowd chant *"FIRE! FIRE! FIRE!"* under all of Act 3B → rip to silence at 0:13 → WHOOMP at 0:14.4.
- **The FIRE twist on Belfort flex:** every Wolf-style luxury beat is swapped for a frugal-wealth gag. Champagne tower → cheap beer. Cash raining → receipts & index-fund statements. Lambo burnout → Honda Civic. That contrast IS the character.
- **Loop bait:** "You're next." at 0:14 pairs with "…there has to be more" at 0:02 — infinite rewatch.
