# WOLF OF FIRE — 15 Second Script

**Format:** Short-form vertical video (TikTok / Reels / Shorts)
**Runtime:** 15 seconds
**Character (FIRE GUY):** Balding, messy rust-orange spiked hair, patchy stubble, heavy-lidded tired eyes, smirk. Dark navy fleece vest over white collared shirt, small flame logo on the chest. Match the reference portrait in every shot — same face, same vest, same flame.

---

## ACT 1 — "THE NORMIE" (0:00 – 0:05)

Washed-out, desaturated color. Fluorescent office lighting. Slow, defeated pace.

**[0:00 – 0:02]** *Medium shot. FIRE GUY at a gray cubicle, shoulders slumped, staring at a spreadsheet. Same tired eyes from the reference, but without the smirk.*

> **FIRE GUY** *(flat, monotone V.O.)*
> "If I just save a little every month… maybe I can retire by sixty-five."

**[0:02 – 0:04]** *Quick cuts: sad desk salad, 2% raise email, a calendar flipping years — 2030… 2040… 2050.*

**[0:04 – 0:05]** *He sighs. Slumps harder. Screen dims to near-black.*

> **FIRE GUY** *(whisper)*
> "…there has to be more."

---

## ACT 2 — "THE SPARK" (0:05 – 0:08)

**[0:05 – 0:06]** *A glowing flame logo ignites on his phone screen. Whip-pan to his face. Color floods back in. Pupils dilate. The smirk from the reference image appears.*

> **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** **F.I.R.E. — Financial Independence, Retire Early**

**[0:06 – 0:08]** *Montage in rapid cuts, beat-synced: index fund chart rocketing, 4% rule graphic, expense column getting slashed, savings rate hitting 70%. Flame trail follows every cut.*

> **FIRE GUY** *(energy rising)*
> "Wait — I don't have to wait **forty years**?!"

---

## ACT 3 — "WOLF OF FIRE" (0:08 – 0:15)

Full saturation. Gold-hour light. Wolf-of-Wall-Street energy — chest mic, pacing, pointing at camera. Crowd roaring off-screen.

**[0:08 – 0:10]** *Hard cut. FIRE GUY on a stage, same fleece vest, same flame on the chest, mic in hand. Confetti. He is ON.*

> **FIRE GUY** *(shouting, pointing at camera)*
> "I'M NEVER GOING BACK TO THAT CUBICLE!"

**[0:10 – 0:12]** *Crowd of normies in office clothes holding up their phones, flame logos glowing back at him like lighters at a concert.*

> **FIRE GUY**
> "Cut the expenses! Crank the savings rate! **LIGHT. IT. UP.**"

*(He pumps his fist on each word. Flames whoosh up behind him on "LIGHT IT UP.")*

**[0:12 – 0:14]** *Tight close-up — exact framing of the reference portrait. Same smirk. Same heavy eyes. But now the flame on his vest is **roaring**, spilling upward past his collar.*

> **FIRE GUY** *(low, Jordan-Belfort cadence)*
> "They told me retire at sixty-five.
> I retired at **thirty-two**."

**[0:14 – 0:15]** *He leans into camera. Whispers.*

> **FIRE GUY**
> "You're next."

*Smash cut to black. Flame logo ignites center-frame with a* **WHOOSH.**

> **ON-SCREEN TEXT:** **JOIN THE FIRE.**

---

## Production Notes

- **Character consistency:** every shot must match the reference portrait — balding crown, rust-orange spiked tufts, stubble, heavy-lidded eyes, navy fleece vest, white collar, flame chest logo. Do not restyle him.
- **Color arc:** desaturated → neutral → saturated/golden. Sell the transformation with grade, not just wardrobe.
- **Audio arc:** muted office hum → single match-strike SFX at 0:05 → rising synth → full crowd roar by 0:10.
- **Pacing:** Act 1 is slow (2 cuts). Act 3 is fast (6+ cuts). The contrast IS the joke.
- **Hook rewatch:** closing "You're next." pairs with the opening "…there has to be more." — loops clean for the algorithm.
